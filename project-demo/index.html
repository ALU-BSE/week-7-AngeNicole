About Full Stack Conf

Join developers from around the world for an intensive day of learning about full-stack development. From frontend frameworks to backend services, we cover it all.

Experience hands-on workshops, networking opportunities, and gain practical knowledge that will accelerate your development career and keep you ahead of industry trends.
